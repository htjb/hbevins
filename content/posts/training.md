I'm currently in week 16 of my Manchester marathon training plan. I started it at a bit of strange time so it's week 16 of 21. It's a deload week and it's needed. My weekly mileage has been building up over the last couple of weeks, and it hit 59 km last week with a 27 km long run.

I have been quite tired and struggling to concentrate at work a bit. Have to manage the fatigue, eat well and try and get some good sleep. 

It can be quite challenging balancing marathon training with everything else happening in my life. Especially during winter. Have to really think about when I run. It's too dark in the evenings to run at the moment, and I'm not a big fan of getting up at 6am to go out and run before my half an hour bike ride into the office.

I'm typically spending Tuesday, Wednesday and Thursday running during lunch which means I'm spending two hours on my lunch break! I've got over being embarrassed walking around the office in my running kit.

Occasionally I will run to work or back from work. It's about 6 km and I can squeeze a few extra kilometers out of the journey if I try.

Only a few more weeks left. I have about 30 km and a gym session to do this week and a few more big weeks then it's taper for the marathon in Manchester. My partner and I are both running it, so we are training together. She is a good motivator!
